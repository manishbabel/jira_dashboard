Learning Agile Scrum is easy and makes us happy

We learn through the Scrum process diagram which has 4 Ceremonies, 3 Roles, and 4 Artifacts. The Agile Manifesto teaches us to value people over tools

Practicing Scrum with the flawed JIRA tool makes us sad

JIRA causes cognitive overload because it does not fully model the Scrum process. Jira supports only 3 of 4 ceremonies and none of the roles so we must mentally map the scrum process diagram to Jira, juggling gaps and work-arounds in our head. Can you believe JIRA doesn't support Scrum's retrospective ceremony? Furthermore, JIRA is text driven with minimal static visualization and certainly no interactive visualization.

So we made the Scrum Visualizer to be happy again

We visualize the mapping from scrum diagram to JIRA and the retrospective and the roles. No more filling in the gaps and imagining Scrum. Start living scrum joy with the Scrum Visualizer."
